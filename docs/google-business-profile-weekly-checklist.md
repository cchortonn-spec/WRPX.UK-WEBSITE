# Google Business Profile Weekly Checklist

This workflow supports local SEO without changing website code.

## One-time setup (do this first)
- Verify ownership of your Google Business Profile.
- Fill in all business details (phone, website, hours, service areas).
- Add services that match website pages (kitchen wrapping, commercial installation, window film).
- Upload at least 20 real project photos.

## Weekly routine (repeat every week)
1. Post one update with real job photos:
   - before/after image
   - area served (for example: Sheffield, Doncaster)
   - service completed
2. Ask 2-3 recent customers for a Google review.
3. Reply to all new reviews (thank them and mention service/location naturally).
4. Add 3-5 new photos from recent projects.

## Monthly quality check
- Ensure opening hours are correct.
- Remove outdated offers.
- Check profile messages and Q&A.
- Compare calls and direction requests month-to-month.

## Content ideas for weekly posts
- Kitchen door wrap before/after
- Worktop wrap transformation
- Commercial graphics install
- Frosted/privacy window film project

## Basic tracking sheet columns
- Week starting date
- Post published (yes/no)
- Photos added (count)
- Reviews requested (count)
- Reviews received (count)
- Replies completed (yes/no)
